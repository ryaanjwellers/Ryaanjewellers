About Ryaan Jewellers

Discover the legacy of excellence and craftsmanship behind our brand

Established with a Vision of Excellence

Ryaan Jewellers is a luxury jewellery brand based in the UAE, specialising in premium gold and diamond collections. With a commitment to purity, craftsmanship, and trust, we offer timeless jewellery designed with love and precision.

Founded in 1995, our journey began with a simple vision: to create jewellery that not only enhances beauty but also becomes a cherished part of your life's most precious moments.

Each piece in our collection is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who combine traditional techniques with contemporary designs, ensuring that every creation is as unique as the person who wears it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our jewellery and services

What is the purity of gold used in your jewellery?

We use 18K and 22K gold for our jewellery collections, ensuring the highest purity and quality. Each piece is hallmarked to guarantee its authenticity.

Do you offer custom jewellery design services?

Yes, we specialize in custom jewellery design. Our expert designers will work with you to create a unique piece that reflects your personal style and requirements.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn jewellery in its original condition. Custom pieces and engraved items are final sale unless there is a manufacturing defect.

Do you provide jewellery authentication certificates?

All our diamond and gemstone jewellery comes with authentication certificates from reputable gemological laboratories. Gold jewellery is hallmarked for purity assurance.

How can I care for my jewellery?

We recommend storing jewellery in a soft pouch or box, avoiding contact with chemicals, and cleaning with a soft cloth. We also offer professional cleaning services at our stores.
